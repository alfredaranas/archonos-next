📚 Your Path to Becoming a Sonographer

1

Learn the Basics (Free)

Study ultrasound physics and anatomy using free YouTube channels and Khan Academy.

2

Get Clinical Experience

Volunteer or shadow at a hospital imaging department.

3

Pass ARDMS Exam

Register at ardms.org and take the SPI physics exam ($200) plus your specialty exam ($250).

4

Get Hired!

Apply to hospitals, imaging centers, and physician offices. Median salary starts at $89,340/year.
